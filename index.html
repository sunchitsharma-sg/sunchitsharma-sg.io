The Product Development Journey

From idea to launch, here's how we build your startup step-by-step.

  • Schedule

  • Discovery

  • Design

  • Development

  • Launch

1. Schedule a Call

This is the first step in bringing your idea to life. We set up a short introductory call to explore your vision and understand what you’re looking to build.

  • Clarify your business idea and core objectives
  • Discuss technical feasibility and potential roadmap
  • Walk through our process and timelines
  • Optionally sign an NDA to protect your IP

2. Discovery

This is the most important phase of the journey. We align on the problem, define your product strategy, and gather everything needed to move into design and development.

  • Create a product roadmap and milestone plan
  • Define tech architecture and user flows
  • Deliverables: wireframes, clickable prototype, scope document
  • Duration: 14–21 business days

3. Design

We turn ideas into visuals. This stage focuses on UI/UX design that matches your product's purpose and user needs.

  • UI/UX mockups and design system
  • High-fidelity visuals and branding assets
  • Tools used: Figma, Adobe Photoshop
  • Duration: 20 business days

4. Development

Now we start building. We use agile sprints to develop your MVP, iterating every 2 weeks for continuous improvement.

  • Frontend: React, React Native
  • Backend: Node.js, Python, TypeScript
  • Weekly demos and progress tracking
  • Duration: 40–60 business days

5. Launch & Analytics

Time to go live! We help you deploy your MVP to production and gather product usage insights to inform future versions.

  • Deployment to AWS, setup monitoring and error tracking
  • Track user behavior using Mixpanel and session tools
  • Provide performance reports and improvement roadmap
  • Duration: 7 business days
