Your Privacy Matters

At Silvergauge, we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring that your personal information is handled in a safe and responsible manner. This policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your data.

We may collect personal information such as your name, email address, phone number, and any other details you provide through our website or communications.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used to:

  • Respond to your queries or provide services

  • Send updates, newsletters, or promotional content (only with consent)

3. Sharing of Data

We do not sell or rent your personal data. We may share information with trusted third-party services solely for the purposes of delivering services to you.

4. Cookies

Our website may use cookies to enhance user experience. You can adjust your browser settings to decline cookies if preferred.

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to ensure your personal data is secure and protected against unauthorized access or loss.

7. Changes to This Policy

Silvergauge may update this privacy policy periodically. We recommend reviewing this page regularly to stay informed of any changes.
